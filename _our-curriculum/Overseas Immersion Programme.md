<h3>Past Trips</h3>
<p><strong>Overseas Trips 2024</strong> 
<br>Students from our Regional Studies Programme (RSP) and our Talent Development
Programme in Social Innovation (TDSI) embarked on an overseas learning
journey to Indonesia in early March, 2024. The RSP students started in
Yogyakarta, before joining the TDSI students in Surabaya. Read more about
their trip at the google sites below!</p>
<p>RSP: https://rspincedar.com
<br>TDSI: <a href="https://sites.google.com/students.edu.sg/socialinnovationinsurabaya2024" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://sites.google.com/students.edu.sg/socialinnovationinsurabaya2024</a>
