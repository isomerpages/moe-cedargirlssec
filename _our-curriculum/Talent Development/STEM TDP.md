<h4><u>About</u></h4>
<p>The STEM Talent Development Programme aims to nurture learners with a
passion for STEM through a curiosity-driven approach that inspires exploration,
cultivates creativity, and empowers innovation. Students build disciplinary
knowledge, strengthen critical and analytical thinking skills, and cultivate
a sense of responsibility to make informed decisions and contribute meaningfully
to society.</p>
<p></p>
<h4><u>Key Programs/Competitions</u></h4>
<p><strong>STEM Tier 1 - Experience</strong>
</p>
<p>Students explore hands-on and inquiry-based learning experiences to spark
interest and curiosity in STEM. Examples include learning about aerodynamics
through drones, culinary science, and robotics.&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Research and Innovation Opportunities</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Students have the opportunity to engage in authentic research and innovation
experiences, working on real-world problems and presenting their findings
on external platforms.</p>
<p>Some of these experiences include:
<br>• MOE Science Mentorship Programme
<br>• NTU Nanyang Research Programme (Junior Researcher)
<br>• MOE Problem-X Challenge
<br>• National STEM Championship
<br>• International Mathematical Modelling Challenge</p>
<p></p>
<ol start="2">
<li>
<p><strong>Advanced Problem-Solving and Olympiad Platforms</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Students further develop their conceptual understanding and analytical
reasoning through participation in national Olympiads.</p>
<p>Some of these experiences include: <strong><br></strong> • Singapore Junior
Biology Olympiad
<br>• Singapore Junior Chemistry Olympiad
<br>• Singapore Junior Physics Olympiad
<br>• Singapore Mathematical Olympiad</p>
