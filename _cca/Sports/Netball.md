<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
<br>The Cedar Netball Team strives to nurture in members a passion for the
sport and personal growth in terms of character building and technical
skills. Guided by strong team spirit, diligence and resilience, Cedar Netballers
seek to become the best version of themselves and pursue excellence. Besides
national competitions, Cedar Netball further provides opportunities for
greater exposure, skills development and team bonding through intra-CCA
Internal League, external tournaments and friendly matches.</p>
