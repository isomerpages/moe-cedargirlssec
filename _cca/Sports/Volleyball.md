<p><strong>ABOUT</strong> 
<br>The Cedar Volleyball Team aims to develop perseverance, commitment and
a strong desire to succeed. The CCA has sustained excellent achievements
in the recent National Schools’ Volleyball competitions. Our team always
strives for excellence and demonstrates passion, perseverance and team-work
through our regular training sessions. On the whole, the CCA strives to&nbsp;give
its members many opportunities to learn to be committed young ladies, with
the drive to pursue the best in themselves.</p>
