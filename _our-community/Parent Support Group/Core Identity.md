<p>The Cedar PSG is a collaborative team of parents dedicated to supporting
our daughters' development. We act as a vital bridge between the home and
the classroom, ensuring parents are well-informed about the school's learning
environment.</p>
<h4>Vision and Mission </h4>
<p>Vision: Hand-in-hand with Cedar to unlock the full potential of our daughters</p>
<p>Mission: To champion an environment that brings out the best in every
Cedarian, developing resilient leaders grounded in the school's values
of Honesty, Perseverance, and Courtesy.</p>
<h4>Primary Objectives</h4>
<p>Awareness: Helping parents understand the academic and social landscape
of the school.</p>
<p>Connection: Creating opportunities for parents to engage with their daughters’
school experiences.</p>
<p>Community: Facilitating bonding between parents through shared experiences.</p>
<p>Support: Providing "tangible assistance" by volunteering for school-run
events.</p>
