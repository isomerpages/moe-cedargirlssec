<p>Every year, Cedar Girls' PSG plays an active role in the school's calendar
of events. Here's what we do:</p>
<p><strong>Participate in school events</strong>&nbsp;such as Induction Ceremony,
Speech Day, Sports Day, National Day celebration and Teachers' Day celebration.</p>
<p><strong>Organise various activities</strong>&nbsp;such as parent-child
bonding sessions, fun family outings, parent-get-together sessions and
parenting workshops.</p>
<p><strong>Join the Parent Befriender-Scholar Programme</strong>&nbsp;where
international scholars in the school are paired with a parent befriender
and Cedarian to help them adapt to life in Singapore.</p>
<p><strong>Organise the annual Youth Day Carnival</strong>&nbsp;which is
a fun-filled day for our daughters to celebrate, unwind and enjoy an array
of games and food with their friends.</p>
