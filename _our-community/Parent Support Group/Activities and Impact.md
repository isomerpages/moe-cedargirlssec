<p>The Cedar PSG is organized into sub-committees to better support the school's
various programs. While the specific names can evolve slightly year-to-year
based on the school's needs, the four primary sub-committee areas typically
include:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Parenting Engagement:</strong> Focuses on organizing educational
talks (such as the to help parents navigate their daughters' secondary
school journey.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Parent Befriender:</strong> Coordinates the Befriender program
for scholars.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Family Bonding:</strong> Leads initiatives like "Bonding with Dads"
and Mother’s Day activities to strengthen the relationship between parents
and their daughters.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Gift and Welfare (School events):</strong>&nbsp; Coordinates volunteers
for student-centric events such as the Year 1 Orientation, Youth Day celebrations,
Staff Day and Sec 4 Graduation gift.</p>
</li>
</ol>
