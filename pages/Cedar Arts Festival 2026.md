<h4><strong>Cedar Arts Festival 2026: Rhythm &amp; Radiance</strong></h4>
<p>Step into Rhythm &amp; Radiance, where Cedar’s performing arts come alive!
From Choir, Band, Dance, Guzheng, Handbells and Drama, to special guest
performers - including our Cedar Alumni Band taking the spotlight for the
very first time - this promises to be a celebration of Cedarian talent
and identity.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Net proceeds will go towards enriching our iconic Cedar track, making
this a gift to generations of Cedarians to come.</p>
